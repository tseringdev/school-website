About Our School

Ngagyur Memorial School is committed to providing a balanced education that nurtures knowledge, discipline, and compassion. Our mission is to guide students toward academic excellence while preserving cultural heritage and spiritual values.

Through dedicated teachers, a supportive learning environment, and meaningful activities, we help students develop confidence, responsibility, and the skills needed for a bright future.
